#### Payment Service Providers

Utexo allows crypto PSPs to offer instant, low-cost USDT payments with Bitcoin-level security. By abstracting Lightning operations and RGB into a single SDK/API, PSPs integrate without running nodes or managing liquidity.

#### Benefits for PSPs

- A Bitcoin-native USDT payment rail
- Lower operational costs with predictable fees
- Reduced smart contract and chain risk
- A future-proof settlement layer

#### How Utexo Helps PSPs

- Instant USDT Checkout and Merchant Payments
- Global Stablecoin Settlement on Bitcoin
- Cross-Border and B2B Payments
- PSP-to-PSP and Internal Settlement
- On/Off-Ramp and Chain Consolidation
- Micropayments and High-Frequency Payouts

### Exchanges

Utexo enables exchanges to add Bitcoin as a first-class USDT settlement network, abstracting Lightning and RGB into a single production-ready API.

- Faster Internal Settlement and Capital Efficiency
- Higher-Velocity BTC/USDT Trading

- Reduced Infrastructure Complexity

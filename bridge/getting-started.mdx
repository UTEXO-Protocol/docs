This guide walks you through both directions of a bridge transfer: depositing USDT from Ethereum onto the Bitcoin RGB layer, and withdrawing it back. The same flow applies to Tron as a source network.

<Info>
  All transfers are atomic and non-custodial. The Utexo Bridge Enclave Signer holds private keys exclusively inside an AWS Nitro Enclave — they never exist outside the hardware boundary.
</Info>

## Prerequisites

Before initiating a transfer, ensure you have the following ready.

**MetaMask (browser extension)**
- ETH balance to cover Ethereum gas fees.
- USDT balance for the amount you want to bridge.

**IRIS Wallet (mobile app)**
- BTC balance to cover the RGB transaction fee on the Bitcoin side.
- Your IRIS Wallet connected to the Bridge. You can connect it in two ways:
  - Click **CONNECT RGB WALLET** at the bottom of the bridge form.
  - Open the wallet list in the top-right corner of the bridge page and click the power button next to **RGBWallet**.

## Ethereum → Bitcoin (RGB)

This direction moves USDT from Ethereum to the Bitcoin RGB layer, where you receive an equivalent TUSDT token.

### Step 1 — Enter the transfer details

On [bridge.utexo.com](https://bridge.utexo.com), set:
- **Send:** Blockchain – Ethereum, Token – USDT.
- **Receive:** Blockchain – Bitcoin (RGB), Token – TUSDT.

In the **Amount** field, enter the number of USDT tokens to transfer.

In the **Destination** field, paste an RGB invoice generated in IRIS Wallet:
1. Open IRIS Wallet and ensure your BTC balance can cover the network fee.
2. Select **Receive assets**.
3. Generate an invoice for the TUSDT asset and copy it.
4. Paste the invoice into the **Destination** field on the Bridge.

### Step 2 — Review and confirm

After filling in the form, a **Transaction Preview** appears with:
- **You will receive** — estimated TUSDT amount after fees.
- **Estimated gas fee** — live Ethereum network gas cost.
- **Bridge commission** — fixed percentage fee charged by Utexo.

Click **Transfer**. MetaMask will prompt you through two confirmation steps:

1. **Approve token spending** — Authorises the Bridge contract to spend your USDT. Click **Confirm**.
2. **Confirm the transaction** — Wait 2–10 seconds for the second MetaMask prompt. Review the details and click **Confirm** to submit.

### Step 3 — Wait for TUSDT to arrive

Open IRIS Wallet and monitor your TUSDT balance. Settlement time depends on Bitcoin network congestion. During periods of high congestion, confirmation may take longer than usual.

<Tip>
  In IRIS Wallet, a completed Ethereum → Bitcoin transfer is highlighted in **green** (asset credit).
</Tip>

## Bitcoin (RGB) → Ethereum

This direction burns TUSDT on the Bitcoin RGB layer and releases the equivalent USDT on Ethereum.

### Step 1 — Enter the transfer details

On [bridge.utexo.com](https://bridge.utexo.com), set:
- **Send:** Blockchain – Bitcoin (RGB), Token – TUSDT.
- **Receive:** Blockchain – Ethereum, Token – USDT.

In the **Amount** field, enter the amount of TUSDT you want to withdraw.

In the **Destination address** field, enter your Ethereum wallet address (for example, from MetaMask).

Review the **Transaction Preview**:
- **You will receive** — estimated USDT amount after fees.
- **Estimated gas fee** and **Commission**.

Click **Transfer**.

### Step 2 — Pay the RGB invoice

The Bridge generates an RGB invoice displayed as a popup with a QR code. Open IRIS Wallet:

1. Navigate to your **TUSDT** token.
2. Tap **Send**.
3. Tap the **QR scanner** icon and scan the QR code from the Bridge popup.
4. Review the transfer details and tap **Send** to confirm.

### Step 3 — Monitor the transaction status

After sending, the transaction appears in IRIS Wallet with the status **WAITING_COUNTERPARTY**.

Refresh your wallet (tap the refresh icon). Once the Bitcoin network picks up the transaction, the status changes to **WAITING_CONFIRMATIONS**. When confirmations are complete, the USDT is released to your Ethereum address.

<Tip>
  In IRIS Wallet, a completed Bitcoin → Ethereum transfer is highlighted in **red** (asset deduction from the RGB layer).
</Tip>

## Fees

| Fee type | Description |
|---|---|
| **Ethereum gas fee** | Variable; priced at live market rates at the time of signing. |
| **Bridge commission** | Fixed percentage of the transferred amount, charged by Utexo. Shown in the Transaction Preview before you confirm. |
| **Bitcoin RGB fee** | Small BTC amount deducted from your IRIS Wallet to settle the RGB transaction on-chain. |

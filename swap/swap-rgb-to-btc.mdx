## What You Will Accomplish

This guide walks you through converting an RGB asset (such as RGB USDT or SatoshiRamen) back into native Bitcoin (BTC) using the Utexo swap interface and Tribe Wallet. By the end you will have:

- Sent an RGB asset from Tribe Wallet via an RGB invoice
- Received native BTC at a Bitcoin address you control
- Confirmed the transaction status in both interfaces

<Info>
  Looking to go the other direction? See [Swap BTC to RGB](/swap/swap-btc-to-rgb).
</Info>

## How RGB → BTC Transfers Work

When you swap RGB to BTC:

1. You set the RGB token to send and the BTC amount to receive in the Utexo interface
2. Utexo generates an **RGB invoice** for you to pay from Tribe Wallet
3. You open Tribe Wallet, scan or paste the invoice, and confirm the send
4. Utexo receives the RGB asset and sends the equivalent BTC to the address you provided
5. Your Bitcoin wallet receives the BTC

The RGB side of the swap is validated client-side by Tribe Wallet before broadcasting. Utexo acts as the swap counterparty and releases BTC upon confirmed receipt of the RGB asset.

## Prerequisites

Before you start:

- **Tribe Wallet** (mobile app) with an **RGB asset balance** to swap (e.g. RGB USDT or SatoshiRamen)
- **A Bitcoin wallet address** where you want to receive BTC (Taproot address recommended — see [What is Bitcoin Taproot Address](/swap/what-is-a-bitcoin-taproot-address))
- Sufficient RGB asset balance to cover the swap amount

<Warning>
  Ensure your Tribe Wallet has an adequate RGB asset balance **before** initiating the swap. The RGB invoice generated by Utexo is time-limited — if Tribe Wallet does not send within the validity window, the swap will fail and you will need to start again.
</Warning>

## Step 1: Enter the Transfer Details in Utexo

In the Utexo swap interface:

1. Set **You send** to the RGB token you want to swap (for example, SatoshiRamen)
2. Set **You receive** to **BTC** (Bitcoin)
3. Enter the amount of RGB tokens to send
4. Click **Swap** to proceed

Review the preview details including:
- The estimated BTC amount you will receive
- The swap fee
- The estimated completion time

5. Click **Confirm** to generate the RGB invoice

<Info>
  The preview is an estimate. The final BTC amount may vary slightly based on network fees at the time of settlement.
</Info>

## Step 2: Copy Your BTC Receive Address

Utexo needs a Bitcoin address to send BTC to once it receives your RGB asset.

1. Copy a BTC receive address from your Bitcoin wallet
2. Paste it into the **BTC receive address** field in Utexo

<Warning>
  Use a **Taproot (P2TR) address** where possible. Standard Bitcoin addresses are also accepted, but Taproot addresses offer better privacy. See [What is Bitcoin Taproot Address](/swap/what-is-a-bitcoin-taproot-address) for more.
</Warning>

## Step 3: Pay the RGB Invoice in Tribe Wallet

Utexo will display an **RGB invoice** (as a QR code and a string). In Tribe Wallet:

1. Open **Other assets** (bottom navigation)
2. Select the RGB asset you are sending
3. Tap **Send**
4. Scan the QR code from the Utexo screen, or paste the invoice string manually
5. Tribe Wallet will pre-fill the recipient invoice details — confirm the asset and amount
6. **Swipe to send** to broadcast the RGB transfer

<Warning>
  Double-check the asset and amount before swiping. RGB transfers are irreversible once broadcast.
</Warning>

## Step 4: Check Transaction Status

After sending from Tribe Wallet:

- In **Tribe Wallet**: the transaction appears with a status label (e.g. "Pending" or "Sent"). Refresh to see updates.
- In **Utexo**: once the RGB transfer is confirmed, Utexo releases BTC to your address. The swap status updates to **Completed**.
- In your **Bitcoin wallet**: the incoming BTC transaction will appear, typically within 1–3 Bitcoin confirmations.

<Info>
  RGB confirmation times depend on the Bitcoin block time. On mainnet, expect 10–30 minutes for full settlement.
</Info>

**Expected result:** Your Bitcoin wallet shows an incoming BTC transaction for the swapped amount minus fees.

## Troubleshooting

| Issue | Likely cause | Fix |
|---|---|---|
| Swap stuck after sending from Tribe Wallet | RGB transfer not yet confirmed on Bitcoin | Wait for Bitcoin confirmations; check status in Utexo |
| BTC not received after swap completes | Wrong BTC address entered | Verify the address in Utexo; contact support if BTC was sent to wrong address |
| Tribe Wallet shows invoice as expired | Invoice timed out before payment | Start a new swap in Utexo and pay the new invoice promptly |
| RGB balance unchanged after send | Transfer failed client-side validation | Check Tribe Wallet for error details; regenerate the invoice and retry |
| Swap shows wrong BTC amount | Fee estimation changed between preview and confirmation | The final amount is shown at confirmation step; always confirm before swiping |

<Info>
  If your swap is stuck or you believe BTC has been lost, contact Utexo support with your swap transaction ID from the Utexo interface.
</Info>

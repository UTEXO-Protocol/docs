To use Utexo Swap, your Tribe Wallet needs a small BTC balance to cover Bitcoin transaction fees and to create UTXOs for receiving RGB assets. This guide explains how to acquire BTC and transfer it to your wallet.

## Step 1: Acquire BTC

You can purchase BTC through a centralised exchange or a fiat onramp service. Use a platform available in your region that supports withdrawals to an external Bitcoin address.

## Step 2: Withdraw BTC to Tribe Wallet

Once you have BTC on an exchange or onramp service:

1. Open Tribe Wallet and copy your **Bitcoin receive address** (a Taproot `bc1p...` address)
2. On your exchange, navigate to **Withdraw** and select **Bitcoin (BTC)**
3. Paste your Tribe Wallet address as the destination
4. Enter the withdrawal amount and confirm

<Note>
  A small BTC amount is sufficient to get started. You need enough to cover transaction fees for UTXO creation and swap operations (typically a few thousand satoshis).
</Note>

## Step 3: Create UTXOs

After your BTC arrives in Tribe Wallet, you must create UTXOs before you can receive RGB assets:

1. Go to **Other assets** (bottom navigation)
2. Tap **Create UTXOs**
3. Select the number of UTXOs to create
4. Confirm the transaction

See [What Is a UTXO](/swap/what-is-utxo) for a full explanation of why this step is required.
